The Stolen Sweetroll

If stolen, you would be disappointed.

Ingredients

Roll

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup caster sugar
  • 1 cup maple syrup or honey
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 60g butter, melted
  • 1 vanilla pod, scraped
  • 3 tsp cinnamon powder

Glaze

  • 1 vanilla pod, scraped
  • 1 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup icing sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400F and grease bundt tin with butter.
  2. Combine baking ingredients and mix well. Pour into bundt tin and bake for 45 minutes.
  3. For the glaze, combine melted butter, sugar, and vanilla in a small bowl and whip until well bleneded.
  4. Drizzle glaze on sweetrolls, and wait for the icing to harden before eating.
