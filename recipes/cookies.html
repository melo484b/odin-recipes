Chocolate Chip Cookies

These chocolate chip cookies are crispy and gooey. A true delight for the taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 1-1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp Baking soda
  • 1 cup Unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup Brown sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 tbsp Milk
  • 1-1/4 tbsp Vanilla extract
  • 2 cups Semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Whisk salt, flour, and baking soda together
  2. Cream butter, sugar, and brown sugar in a separate bowl.
  3. Add egg, milk, and vanilla extract to the bowl with the creamed butter and sugar and whisk until combined.
  4. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and stir until mixed well. Add chocolate chips.
  5. Refridgerate dough until firm. Preheat oven to 350F and line baking tray with parchment paper.
  6. Add tablespoon-sized scoops of dough to the baking tray, spaced apart by about 4 inches. Bake for about 12 minutes.
