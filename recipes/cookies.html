Chocolate Chip Cookies

These chocolate chip cookies are crispy and gooey. A true delight for the taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 1-1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp Baking soda
  • 1 cup Unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup Brown sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 tbsp Milk
  • 1-1/4 tbsp Vanilla extract
  • 2 cups Semisweet chocolate chips
